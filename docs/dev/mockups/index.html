Design slate (resolved)

Three directions were explored for the Ronin Advisory Group front page. A — Editorial masthead was chosen and has shipped to the live site. B and C are archived as recorded alternatives, not deleted — both retain theory value for a future contributor wondering why the editorial register won out. A and B converged independently on warm paper and oxidized red; that convergence was treated as a brand-color signal, not a coincidence, and landed in the shipped palette.

This page is gitignored at site/_mockups/ and lives in the repo at docs/dev/mockups/. It is a record of the slate's arc, not an under-review screen. Decisions and reasoning live in docs/dev/plans/04-design-system-lock-in.md.
